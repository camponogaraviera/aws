# About

[AWS Elemental MediaConvert](https://aws.amazon.com/mediaconvert/) is a professional-grade, file-based video transcoding service used to convert **on-demand** video content (e.g., MP4 uploads) into adaptive bitrate streaming formats (e.g., HLS, DASH, CMAF) and other codecs, including AVC (H.264 or MPEG-4 Part 10), HEVC (H.265), and more.
