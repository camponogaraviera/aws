# About

[AWS CodeDeploy](https://aws.amazon.com/codedeploy/faqs/) is a fully managed deployment service that automates software deployments to a variety of compute services, such as Amazon EC2, AWS Fargate, Lambda, and on-premises servers. It supports rolling updates, blue (current production)/green (staging/new version) deployments, and automatic rollback (if issues arise with the new version), helping ensure high availability and minimal downtime during updates.
